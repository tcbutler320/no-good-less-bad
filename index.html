Information Security

In my professional work, I've helped to secure client networks as a cyber risk consultant and penetration tester in Deloitte's Government & Public Services Cybersecurity Practice. In my free time, I conduct freelance research to identify vulnerabilities in software and coordinate disclosures with vendors to mitigate risk. Over the years, I've disclosed dozens of vulnerabilities in open-source projects, web applications, and IoT or embedded devices, culminating in several common vulnerabilities and exposures (CVEs). Some of my most recent research includes CVE-2021-38701, CVE-2021-3441, CVE-2021-35956, and a nomination to the 2021 Motorolasolutions Bug Bounty Hall of Fame.
