Welcome

{% include image.html url="/images/me.jpg" width=200 align="left" %} Welcome to my academic website! Here you can find updates on my academic work, security research, and blog entries on information security. I'm currently a graduate student at Georgetown University in the Security Studies Program (SSP) at the Walsh School of Foreign Service (SFS). My focus is on the intersection of emerging technology and national security, with an anticipated certificate in Eurasian, Russian and East European Studies (CERES). Prior to studying at Georgetown, I obtained my undergraduate degree in Security and Risk Analysis at The Pennsylvania State University.




Vulnerability Research

In addition to my academic work, I conduct freelance research to identify vulnerabilities in software and coordinate disclosures with vendors to mitigate risk. Over the years, I've disclosed dozens of vulnerabilities in open-source projects, web applications, and IoT or embedded devices, culminating in several common vulnerabilities and exposures (CVEs). Some of my most recent research includes CVE-2021-38701, CVE-2021-3441, and CVE-2021-35956.
